Vibgyor Paints India Limited is the third largest paint manufacturing company in the country. We manufacture paint for both interior and exterior use. We have earned a reputation for innovation with new products such as exterior paints designed to reflect heat, anti-bacterial, water resistant paint etc. Vibgyor Paints has a consistent track record of being one of the fastest growing paint companies, quarter-on-quarter, for the past three years. From an annual sales turnover of Rs 25 Lakhs, 3 years ago, their business revenues are in excess of Rs 2000 crores today.
